Pst Kenneth Hagin

A Great Preacher and evangelist


In 1936, he founded his first non-denominational church,Hagin founded Rhema Bible Training College in 1974, was greater preacher and Authur of more than 40 christain books,God used him greatly to perform various miracles during his time

Here's a time line of Pst Kenneth Hagin

  • 1917 - born in McKinney Texas, the son of Lillie Viola Drake Hagin and Jess Hagin
  • 1933 - he converted to Christianity
  • 1934 - He said he was raised from his deathbed by a revelation of "faith in God's Word
  • 1936 - he founded his first non-denominational church
  • 1938 - he married Oretha Rooker,His belove wife
  • 1949 - Hagin began an itinerant ministry as a Bible teacher and evangelis
  • 1963 - he formed the Kenneth E. Hagin Evangelistic Association
  • 1966 - He started selling his sermons on reel-to-reel tape
  • 1967 - he began a regular radio broadcast that still continues as "Faith Seminar of the Air."
  • 1974 - Hagin opened RHEMA Bible Training College, in Broken Arrow, Oklahoma
  • 1979 - he founded the Prayer and Healing Center to provide a place for the sick to come
  • 1994 -Hagin received an Honorary Doctor of Divinity Degree from Faith Theological Seminary in Tampa, Florida
  • 2003 - Hagin depart to be with the Lord
